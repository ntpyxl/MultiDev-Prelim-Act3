ATTRACTIONS

Discover the attractions within the island of Siquijor!

Cambugahay Falls

Cambugahay Falls is a stunning multi-tiered waterfall known for its turquoise waters, lush surroundings, and inviting natural pools perfect for swimming and rope swinging.

Paliton Beach

Paliton Beach is a serene white-sand beach with crystal-clear waters, swaying palm trees, and breathtaking sunsets, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and photography.

View More
Cantabon Cave

Cantabon Cave is a fascinating underground wonder featuring stunning rock formations, stalactites, and stalagmites. Adventurers explore its narrow passages, underground streams, and natural pools for a thrilling experience.

View More
Old Enchanted Balete Tree

The Old Enchanted Balete Tree is a centuries-old tree surrounded by mystical legends. Its roots form a natural fish spa, where visitors can soak their feet while experiencing the island's rich folklore, spirituality, and enchanting atmosphere.

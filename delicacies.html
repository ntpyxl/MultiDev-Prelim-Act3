DELICACIES

Discover the delicious meals that can be found within the island of Siquijor!

Torta

Torta is a soft, sweet, and spongy native cake from Siquijor, made with eggs, flour, and tuba (fermented coconut wine), giving it a distinct aroma and rich, slightly tangy flavor.

View More
Otap

Otap is a crunchy, flaky, and sugar-coated biscuit made from puff pastry, often enjoyed as a light snack. Its delicate texture makes it crumbly, giving a satisfying crisp with every bite.

View More
Pan Bisaya

Pan Bisaya is a traditional bread from Siquijor, often filled with sweet bukayo (caramelized coconut) or other local ingredients. It has a soft, slightly chewy texture and is a favorite merienda treat.

View More
Banana Crackers

Banana crackers are crispy, thinly sliced banana chips baked or fried to perfection. Lightly sweetened, they offer a delicious crunch and are a popular snack made from locally grown bananas in Siquijor.
